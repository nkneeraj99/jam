Franchise Opportunity

Jamboree started operations in 1993 and created a market for others to follow. It is the oldest organization of its kind with the biggest market share and deepest market penetration. We are a Specialized Preparation, Coaching, Training & Counseling Institute for GMAT, GRE, SAT, TOEFL, IELTS Tests & University Admissions.

Jamboree Admissions Counseling Cell was started in the year 2003; and, since its inception, has produced excellent results. We have placed students in top universities across the globe including Harvard, Wharton, Stanford, MIT, London Business School, Oxford, Cambridge, Columbia, Yale, Cornell, etc.

Meet Jamboree Franchise Owner

Heena Talwar

Franchisee Owner – Jamboree Dehradun

After 12 years of working in various companies and industries, I wanted to start my own business. Since I do not come from a business family with strong financial reserves, I decided to take a franchise of a well-known brand to minimize the financial risks.

I opened Jamboree’s Dehradun franchise in Jan 2011. It’s been a wonderful association till now. I received ample assistance from the head office – right from setup guidelines to faculty hiring and training to making targets and business plans. I was able to start earning the day-to-day operating expenses from the 5th month itself.

Over the years, in Jamboree Dehradun, we have successfully placed numerous students in their dream universities. I am glad I decided to take Jamboree’s franchise. Very few people are able to have job satisfaction alongwith the desired financial gains. Thanks to Jamboree, I am one of them.

Krishna Aryal

Franchisee Owner – Jamboree Nepal

I hail from a business family, our family business being infrastructure development. After my MBA, I was expected to join it. But, I was interested in doing something where I can be independent and actively use my management education. So, my friend and I took a franchise of Eurokids, a chain of playschools. After successfully running a few Eurokids franchises in Kathmandu, we wanted to take franchise of some other education company. After careful consideration, we zeroed in on the study abroad industry and from there, Jamboree was a natural choice.

Our association with Jamboree is almost 5 years old now. The support they provide have exceeded our expectations. I was immensely impressed when they arranged a faculty for our centre in no time even in the unfavorable circumstances after the Nepal earthquake in 2015. The frequent visits by Jamboree officials during the initial phase instilled confidence in us. Over these years, Jamboree’s focus on standardization and the constant emphasis on quality have ensured that Jamboree Nepal students have got admissions in some of the top universities in the world.

Jamboree is now the biggest study abroad company in Nepal, and I am its proud owner.
